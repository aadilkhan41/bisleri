FAQs

Packaged Drinking Water-related:
Is water enriched with minerals good for you?

Yes, mineral water is good for you as it provides hydration and essential minerals such as calcium, magnesium, and sodium. It may also have health benefits such as promoting bone health, lowering blood pressure, improving digestive health, and supporting heart health.

Is Bisleri packaged drinking water?

Yes, Bisleri is mineral water as it contains natural minerals and trace elements.

Does Bisleri packaged drinking water have any health benefits? 

Drinking Bisleri water can be good for health as it is extensively purified and enriched with natural minerals. 

What is the pH of Bisleri?

The pH of Bisleri packaged drinking water ranges from 6 to 8.5.  On the other hand, Bisleri Vedica natural mineral water has a pH level between 7.3 and 7.9, making it naturally alkaline and a soothing antacid option.

What is TDS in water? 

TDS stands for total dissolved solids and affects water's quality, taste, and appearance. A TDS level between 75-150 PPM is considered acceptable for packaged drinking water, whereas natural mineral water has a TDS between 150-700 PPM. Bisleri water maintains a TDS level up to 150 PPM, making it safe to drink.

Does packaged drinking water expire? 

Mineral water bottles usually have an expiry date printed for quality assurance. Additionally, they should be stored away from heat and sunlight, properly sealed.

What is the expiry date of Bisleri water? 

You can check the date of expiration for your bottle on the label or cap of the bottle. The expiry date of Bisleri packaged drinking water varies depending on the size of the bottle/can you are purchasing. For example, The 10-litre can has an expiry date of 3 months, wheras the 20-litre can is best before 1 month.

Bisleri-related:
Is Bisleri 20-litre water can good? 

Yes, Bisleri 20-litre water can is good as it provides a larger quantity of water that is filtered and mineralised. It is ideal for home or office use, serving many people and saving space. 

How many bottles are in a box of Bisleri?

The number of bottles in a box of Bisleri depends on the size of the bottle. For example, a box of 250 ml water bottles contains 24 bottles, a box of 500 ml water bottles contains 24 bottles, a box of 1-litre water bottles contains 12 bottles, and a box of 2-litre water bottles contains 9 bottles.

What size is a Bisleri pack?

A Bisleri pack refers to the size of the bottle that contains the Bisleri water. Bisleri packaged drinking water comes in various sizes ranging from 250 ml to 20 litre. 

Can we reuse Bisleri bottles? 

Bisleri bottles cannot be reused and should be crushed and disposed after use, as mentioned on the label.

How do I return an empty Bisleri bottle? 

You can return an empty Bisleri bottle to the retailer or distributor from where you bought it. Depending on their policy, you may also get a refund or exchange for returning the bottle. Alternatively, you can return an empty Bisleri bottle to a Bottles for Change collection center, which will be recycled into useful products.

Is Bisleri suitable for daily use?

Yes, Bisleri is suitable for daily use as it provides pure, safe, and healthy water enriched with minerals.
